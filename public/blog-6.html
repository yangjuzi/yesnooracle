Top 100 YES/NO Questions: A Comprehensive Guide

❓
Oracle Guide
January 1, 2025 · 15 min read

After years of analyzing thousands of divination sessions, I've compiled the most common YES/NO questions that people ask. These questions reflect our deepest concerns, hopes, and uncertainties. Whether you're seeking guidance for a major life decision or simply curious about what others are asking, this comprehensive guide will help you understand the most pressing questions in our lives.

Love and Relationships

Love and relationships consistently top the list of concerns in divination sessions. These questions often reflect our deepest emotional needs and insecurities.

  • Does he/she love me?
  • Is this relationship meant to be?
  • Should I give them another chance?
  • Will we get back together?
  • Is it time to move on?
  • Will I find true love?
  • Should I trust them?
  • Is this the right person for me?
  • Will our relationship last?
  • Should I propose?

Career and Work

Career-related questions often reflect our professional aspirations and concerns about financial stability and personal growth.

  • Should I take this job offer?
  • Will I get the promotion?
  • Is it time to change careers?
  • Should I start my own business?
  • Will this investment be profitable?
  • Should I ask for a raise?
  • Is this the right career path?
  • Will this project succeed?
  • Should I go back to school?
  • Is this the right time to retire?

Personal Growth

Questions about personal development and self-improvement show our desire for growth and betterment.

  • Am I on the right path?
  • Should I move to a new city?
  • Will I achieve my goals?
  • Is this the right decision?
  • Should I trust my intuition?
  • Will this change be positive?
  • Is it time to let go?
  • Should I take this risk?
  • Will I find happiness?
  • Is this my true purpose?

Health and Well-being

Health-related questions reflect our concerns about physical and mental well-being.

  • Will I recover from this illness?
  • Should I try this treatment?
  • Is this diet right for me?
  • Will this exercise routine help?
  • Should I see a specialist?
  • Is my health improving?
  • Will I overcome this condition?
  • Should I make this lifestyle change?
  • Is this stress affecting my health?
  • Will I achieve my fitness goals?

Family and Friends

Questions about family and friendships often reflect our concerns about relationships and support systems.

  • Should I forgive them?
  • Will our friendship last?
  • Is this person trustworthy?
  • Should I confront them?
  • Will my family support me?
  • Is it time to set boundaries?
  • Should I help them?
  • Will we reconcile?
  • Is this friendship toxic?
  • Should I let them go?

Spiritual and Personal Beliefs

Questions about spirituality and personal beliefs reflect our search for meaning and purpose.

  • Is this my spiritual path?
  • Should I trust my intuition?
  • Will I find inner peace?
  • Is this a sign?
  • Should I follow this guidance?
  • Will I understand my purpose?
  • Is this the right spiritual practice?
  • Should I trust this feeling?
  • Will I find answers?
  • Is this my calling?
