Frequently Asked Questions

Are the dice rolls truly random?

Yes! Our dice roller uses JavaScript's Math.random() function with additional randomization techniques to ensure fair and unpredictable results for every roll.

What dice types are available?

We offer six standard dice types: D4 (1-4), D6 (1-6), D8 (1-8), D10 (1-10), D12 (1-12), and D20 (1-20). These cover most tabletop gaming and decision-making needs.

Can I roll multiple dice at once?

Absolutely! You can roll up to 10 dice simultaneously. The tool shows individual results for each die plus the total sum, perfect for games requiring multiple dice.

Is this suitable for serious gaming?

Yes! Our dice roller is designed for both casual and serious use. The randomization is cryptographically sound and suitable for tabletop RPGs, board games, and competitive play.

How do I use this for decision-making?

Assign numbers to your options (e.g., 1-3 for three choices with a D6), then roll to let chance decide. This removes bias and can help when you're truly undecided between good options.
