Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Yes No Button work?

The button uses a simple random algorithm to generate either "Yes" or "No" answers. Each click gives you a 50/50 chance of either response.

Can I trust the answers for important decisions?

This tool is designed for fun and quick decisions. For important life choices, we recommend using our more comprehensive decision-making tools or consulting with professionals.

Why should I use this instead of flipping a coin?

Our Yes No Button is always available, provides instant feedback, and includes fun visual effects. Plus, you can save your questions for reference!
