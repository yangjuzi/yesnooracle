Decision making is one of the most critical skills we need in life, yet most of us never receive formal training in how to make better choices. Today, we explore evidence-based tools and insights that will transform how you approach important decisions.

Why Decision Science Matters

Every day, we make thousands of decisions. Research shows that the quality of our decisions directly impacts our happiness, success, and well-being.

However, our brains evolved for a different world. The cognitive shortcuts that helped our ancestors survive often work against us in our complex modern environment.

Understanding Cognitive Biases

One of the biggest breakthroughs in decision science has been identifying systematic errors in thinking that lead to poor decisions.

Confirmation Bias

We seek information that confirms what we already believe while ignoring contradictory evidence.

Loss Aversion

Losses feel roughly twice as powerful as gains, leading us to make overly conservative choices.
