What is Pros & Cons Analysis?

Pros & Cons Analysis is a simple but powerful way to break down any decision. You list out all the positives (pros) and negatives (cons) for each option. Seeing everything side by side helps you get out of your head and onto the page‚Äîso you can make a choice with confidence.

Why Use It?

When you're stuck, it's easy to get overwhelmed by feelings or overthink things. Writing out the pros and cons makes your thinking visible. It helps you spot what really matters, see trade-offs clearly, and avoid snap judgments. Plus, it's a great way to talk through decisions with friends, family, or teammates.

When Should You Use It?

How to Use the Pros & Cons Tool
